Chocotorta

Description

With Argentinian origin the Chocotorta is a delicious and easy cake to make.

It takes very few ingredients these being, chocolate cookies, and a cream made combining milk jam or "dulce de leche" with cream cheese.

Ingredients

  • 400 grms Dulce de Leche / Milk Jam
  • 400 grms Cream Cheese
  • 100 ml Coffee
  • 100 ml Water
  • Chocolate cookies (two packages)
  • Optional: Chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. First you must prepare the filling cream
  2. Mix the Dulce de Leche with the cheese cream until smooth
  3. Put the Coffee and water in a dish. This could be replaces with Chocolate Milk if you do not like Coffee.
  4. Inside a high tray place a first layer of cookies after dipping them in the Coffee + Water mix.
  5. On top place a generous layer of the cream. Make sure to cover all of the cookies.
  6. Repeat the steps, place another layer of cookies and on top another layer of cream.
  7. Repeat until there are no more cookies and you cover everything with the cream.
  8. Store in the fridge
